Our Story

Rooted in connection, committed to care

Willow Vale was born from a simple conviction: that every person — regardless of background, circumstance, or the complexity of what they carry — deserves a space where they can breathe, be heard, and begin to heal.

Founded in 2016, we've grown from a single consulting room into a small team of dedicated therapists, united by our commitment to person-centred, trauma-informed care. We believe the therapeutic relationship itself is at the heart of any meaningful change.

Our name reflects our philosophy. The willow bends without breaking — resilient, flexible, deeply rooted. The vale is sheltered: a place of quietness away from life's storms. Together, they describe the kind of space we strive to create for everyone who walks through our door.

We are proud members of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) and work in accordance with their ethical framework.

What We Offer

Support tailored to you

We draw on a range of therapeutic approaches, always led by what works best for you as an individual. Below are the main areas we work in.

Individual
Person-Centred Counselling

Our foundation. Drawing on the work of Carl Rogers, we offer an empathic, non-directive space where you are the expert on your own life. Suitable for anxiety, low mood, grief, life transitions, and more.

Individual
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

A structured, evidence-based approach that explores the links between thoughts, feelings, and behaviour. Particularly effective for anxiety disorders, depression, OCD, and phobias.

Specialist
Trauma-Informed Therapy

Sensitive, paced support for those who have experienced trauma. We use an integrative approach — including elements of EMDR and somatic awareness — to support healing at a deep level.

Relational
Couples Counselling

A supportive space for partners to explore communication, conflict, intimacy, or life changes together. We work with couples at any stage of their relationship.

Young People
Counselling for Young People

Specialist support for 12–18 year olds, using age-appropriate, creative approaches to help young people navigate life's pressures, identity, and emotional wellbeing.

Flexible
Online & Telephone Sessions

We offer secure video and telephone appointments for those who prefer to access support from the comfort of home, or who aren't able to attend in person.

Your Therapist

Meet Ben

Hi, I'm Ben — a qualified, BACP-registered counsellor and the person behind Willow Vale. I set up this practice because I believe everyone deserves a genuinely warm, non-judgmental space to explore what's going on for them.

I work with adults and young people on a wide range of issues — anxiety, low mood, life transitions, relationship difficulties, self-esteem, and more. My approach is primarily person-centred, meaning I follow your lead and work at a pace that feels right for you.

Before training as a therapist I spent years working in fast-paced, high-pressure environments — so I have a real understanding of how stress, burnout, and the weight of modern life can affect us. Whatever brings you here, you'll find an open door.
